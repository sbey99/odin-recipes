Easy Pesto Pasta

Description

This pesto pasta recipe is an easy, quick, and delicious choice for those nights when you just don't feel like cooking. In this recipe we'll be including tomatoes, mushrooms, and shrimp, but you can add anything you like! This recipe is great for both vegans and meat lovers alike. We hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Penne pasta
  • Pesto
  • Grape tomatoes
  • Muhsrooms
  • Shrimp
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Pour pasta into boiling water and boil until al dente
  3. While pasta is boiling wash and cut tomatoes and mushrooms
  4. Pour olive oil into a non-stick pan and add tomatoes, mushrooms, and shrimp
  5. Drain pasta and add to pan with tomatoes, mushrooms and shrimp
  6. Add pesto and stir
  7. Add healthy serving of parmesan cheese
  8. Enjoy!
